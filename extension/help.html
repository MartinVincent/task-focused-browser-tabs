Help

This extension will manage all tabs located on the right-hand side of this tab.

Double-click on any task and all tabs URLs that were saved for this task will be reopened.

When closing a task or switching to another one, then the URLs of all open tabs (and their order) will be memorized.

All tabs that are not in this windows and on the right of this one are not affected.

This extension will try to move to the left (that is, out of the way) all new tabs that might appear when opening links outside browser (e.g. clicking on a link in an email). Just move any tab to the right to make it part of current active task.
